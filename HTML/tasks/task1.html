AVENGERS

Spider-Man

Peter Parker, a high school student, gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He uses his powers to fight crime and protect New York City as Spider-Man.

Iron Man

Tony Stark, a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor, creates a powered suit of armor to save his life and escape captivity. He becomes the superhero Iron Man.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, a frail young man, is enhanced to the peak of human physicality by an experimental serum in order to aid the United States government's efforts during World War II. He becomes the superhero Captain America.
